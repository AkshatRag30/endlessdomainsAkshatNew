Setup Guide
How to Park Your Domain.

From login to live in under 5 minutes. Do this exactly once. After that it runs itself.

01

Log in or create an account

Sign in with your wallet or email. Takes under 30 seconds.

02

Get your domain ( Skip if you own one )

Register a new Web3 domain or import one you already own from any supported provider.

03

Visit the Park page

Navigate to the Parked Domains section from your dashboard.

04

Connect your domain

Select the domain you want to park. One click links it to Endless Domains.

05

Select your template

Choose Individual, Influencer, or Business. Each has a purpose-built layout.

06

IPFS link is created

The platform handles the IPFS connection automatically. Your domain resolves to your branded page on-chain.

07

Your domain is parked and earning

Visitors see your branded page with ads. Revenue flows to your wallet. Nothing more to manage.
